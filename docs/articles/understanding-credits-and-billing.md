# Understanding Credits and Billing

Credits are 2Bot's universal currency for AI-powered features. Here's everything you need to know.

## What Are Credits?
Credits are consumed when you use 2Bot's built-in AI features (text generation, image creation, voice synthesis, etc.). Each AI model has different credit costs.

## Credit Allocation by Plan

| Plan | Credits/Month | Claim Type |
|------|--------------|------------|
| Free | 100 | Daily (3.3/day) |
| Starter | 500 | Daily (17/day) |
| Pro | 2,000 | Monthly |
| Business | 6,000 | Monthly |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | — |

## How Credits Are Used
- **Text (Lite)** — ~1 credit per short conversation
- **Text (Pro)** — ~2 credits per conversation
- **Text (Ultra)** — ~4 credits per conversation
- **Image Generation** — 5-15 credits per image
- **Voice Synthesis** — 2-5 credits per generation

## Checking Your Balance
- Dashboard → Credits page shows your current balance
- The AI widget shows your usage percentage
- You'll get a warning at 80% usage

## Daily vs Monthly Claims
- **Free & Starter**: Credits refresh daily. Unused daily credits don't roll over.
- **Pro & above**: Full monthly allocation granted at cycle start.

## Organization Credits
Organizations have separate credit wallets. The org owner allocates budgets to departments, and department managers can allocate to members.

## Need More Credits?
Upgrade your plan for higher allocations, or contact us about Enterprise pricing for unlimited usage.
