# Account Settings and Security

Manage your 2Bot account settings, security, and preferences.

## Account Settings
Go to **Dashboard → Settings** to manage:
- **Profile** — Update your name and email
- **Password** — Change your password
- **Plan** — View or upgrade your subscription

## Changing Your Password
1. Go to Settings → Password
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter and confirm your new password
4. Click "Update Password"

## Forgot Password?
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Create a new password

## Upgrading Your Plan
1. Go to Settings → Billing or Dashboard → Billing
2. Click "Upgrade" on the desired plan
3. Complete payment via Stripe
4. New plan takes effect immediately

## Deleting Your Account
Contact support@2bot.org to request account deletion. This action is permanent and will:
- Remove all your gateways and plugins
- Cancel any active subscriptions
- Delete your data

## Security Tips
- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your API keys or bot tokens
- Regularly rotate gateway credentials
- Review your audit log for suspicious activity
