# Managing Organizations and Teams

Organizations let you collaborate with your team on 2Bot. Here's how to set up and manage your org.

## Creating an Organization
1. Go to **Dashboard → Organizations**
2. Click **"Create Organization"**
3. Set a name and URL slug
4. Choose an org plan

## Organization Plans

| Plan | Seats | Credits/Month |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Org Free | 3 | 100 |
| Org Starter | 5 | 500 |
| Org Growth | 15 | 2,000 |
| Org Pro | 40 | 5,000 |
| Org Business | 100 | 15,000 |
| Org Enterprise | Unlimited | Custom |

## Inviting Members
1. Go to your Org settings → Members
2. Click **"Invite Member"**
3. Enter their email address
4. Assign a role: Owner, Admin, or Member

## Departments
Departments help organize your team:
- Create departments for different teams
- Assign members to departments
- Allocate credit budgets per department
- Department managers can manage their own team

## Roles
- **Owner** — Full control, billing, delete org
- **Admin** — Manage members, gateways, plugins
- **Member** — Use assigned resources

## Context Switching
Switch between your personal account and org context using the context switcher in the dashboard header. Resources and credits are separate per context.
