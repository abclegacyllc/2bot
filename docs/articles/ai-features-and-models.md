# 2Bot AI Features and Models

2Bot includes built-in AI capabilities powered by leading AI providers. Access them through the AI widget (bottom-right of dashboard).

## Available Models

### Text Generation
- **Lite** — Fast and affordable (powered by GPT-4o-mini / Claude Haiku). Great for simple tasks.
- **Pro** — Balanced quality and speed (powered by GPT-4o / Claude Sonnet). Good for most tasks.
- **Ultra** — Premium quality (powered by Claude Opus). Best for complex reasoning.

### Image Generation
- **Pro** — Standard image generation
- **Ultra** — High-quality image creation

### Voice
- **Voice Pro** — Text-to-speech synthesis
- **Voice Ultra** — Premium voice quality
- **Transcription Lite** — Speech-to-text

## Using the AI Widget
1. Click the **AI icon** (bottom-right corner)
2. Select your model from the model picker
3. Type your message or upload an image
4. Credits are deducted automatically

## Smart Model Routing
2Bot can automatically route your query to the most cost-effective model based on complexity. Enable "Smart Routing" to save credits on simple questions.

## Context Switching
Switch between Personal and Organization contexts to use the appropriate credit wallet.

## Tips for Saving Credits
- Use **Lite** for simple questions and drafting
- Use **Pro** for most work
- Reserve **Ultra** for complex analysis
- Enable Smart Routing for automatic optimization
